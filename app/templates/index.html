Spot the Fake

Learn how to identify AI-generated misinformation during the 2026 Elections.

❌ Misinformation Red Flags

  • Unnatural blinking or robotic voice in video broadcasts.
  • Sensationalist headlines with zero verified source links.
  • Unverified WhatsApp forwards regarding polling dates.

✅ Verification Checklist (ECINET Mandates)

  • Always check for the Official ECI Digital Signature on PDF notifications.
  • Scan the Official QR Code provided on physical election notices.
  • Verify dates directly on eci.gov.in.
